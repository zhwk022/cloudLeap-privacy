Privacy Policy

CloudLeap

This Privacy Policy explains how CloudLeap handles information when you use the iPhone app.

Effective date: 2026-05-25

Chinese version: privacy-policy.zh-CN.html

Summary

CloudLeap is designed as a lightweight offline game. In its current version, the app does not require account registration, does not include advertising SDKs, does not use tracking technologies, and does not send personal data to our servers.

Information We Collect

In the current version of the app, we do not collect personal information through remote servers or third-party analytics services.

The app stores limited gameplay-related data locally on your device only, such as:

  • High scores
  • Sound settings
  • Other basic in-app preferences needed for gameplay

This local data stays on your device and is not used to identify you.

Information We Do Not Collect

In the current version of the app, we do not collect or process:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Precise location
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Payment information
  • Health data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers for tracking purposes

Tracking, Analytics, and Advertising

The app does not currently:

  • Use third-party advertising SDKs
  • Use third-party analytics SDKs
  • Track you across apps or websites
  • Show an App Tracking Transparency permission prompt

Account and Login

The app does not require account creation, login, or profile registration.

Data Sharing

Because we do not collect personal data through the app in its current version, we do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

We may disclose information only if required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.

Data Retention

Locally stored gameplay data remains on your device until:

  • You delete the app
  • You reset the app's local data through device or app-level actions, if available

Because we do not maintain user accounts or remote user databases for the current version, we do not retain personal account records on our servers.

Children's Privacy

The app is a general-audience game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will review the issue promptly.

Security

We design the current version of the app to minimize privacy risk by avoiding unnecessary data collection and by keeping gameplay data local to the device where possible. However, no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

International Users

If you use the app outside your home country or region, you understand that any future updates may be governed by the laws applicable to the jurisdictions in which the app is distributed and operated.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app, legal requirements, or business operations. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
